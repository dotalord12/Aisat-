Academics

Explore our academic programs and courses.

Computer Science

Computer science is the study of algorithms, computational processes, and the principles underlying the design and analysis of computer systems, encompassing both software and hardware aspects, and their applications in various domains such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science.

Information Technology

Information Technology encompasses the study, design, development, implementation, support, and management of computer-based information systems to meet the needs of organizations and individuals..

Computer Engineering

Computer engineering is a discipline that combines aspects of electrical engineering and computer science to design and develop hardware and software systems, including computer systems, networking infrastructure, and embedded systems.
